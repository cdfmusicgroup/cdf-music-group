About CDF Music Group

Built By A Musician.
For Musicians.

Jason Mlakar  ·  Founder & Creative Director

CDF Music Group is an independent live music content platform. We film stripped-down studio sessions and long-form interviews with original artists and distribute the content across YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.

Every artist is original. No tribute acts, no covers. The format is raw and unproduced by design: one room, one take, one honest look at who the artist really is.

In six months the platform has crossed 500K views across all channels and is growing week over week through organic discovery and targeted YouTube advertising.
