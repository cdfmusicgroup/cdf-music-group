About Craig Gerdes

Stories Worth
Telling

Craig Gerdes is a singer-songwriter who crafts original music with the kind of careful attention to lyric and melody that marks a serious songwriter. His performances are intimate by design, built around the song and the story, with nothing extraneous getting in the way.

Drawing from folk, country, and Americana influences, Gerdes has developed a sound that is understated but resonant. The kind of music that improves with repeated listening, revealing new layers in the writing each time through.

Craig Gerdes came to the CDF studio for a live session and long-form interview that stands as one of the more introspective and carefully crafted performances in the platform's catalog.
