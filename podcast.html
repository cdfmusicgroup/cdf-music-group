About the Show

Two Musicians.
One Conversation.

Jam w/ Jason is a long-form interview series built around a simple idea: give original artists the time and space to tell their story properly. No five-minute highlight reel. No press-ready answers. Just a real conversation between two people who care about original music.

Every episode is filmed as part of a CDF studio session and pushed to all major podcast platforms so the conversation reaches listeners wherever they are.

Peer to Peer
Jason is a musician. The conversation feels like two artists talking because it is. No host, no publicist, no agenda.
Long Form
These conversations run as long as they need to. The good stuff usually comes out after the obvious questions are out of the way.
Original Artists Only
Every guest writes and performs their own music. That is the only filter. Genre, career stage, and geography are wide open.
Video and Audio
Watch the full interview on YouTube or listen as a podcast. Same conversation, same depth, your preferred format.
