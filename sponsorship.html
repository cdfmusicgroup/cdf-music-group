The Audience

Who's
Watching

CDF's audience is not passive. These are people who follow artists, attend live shows, seek out original music, and care deeply about the craft. They subscribe, share, and show up.

They are musicians, singer-songwriters, and serious music fans. The kind of people who notice what's in the room on the desk, on the mic stand, on the shelf behind the artist.

Musicians
Active artists who write, record, and perform their own music. They research gear, tools, and services.
Music Fans
People who follow independent artists and seek out content that goes deeper than a highlight reel.
Midwest Core
Strong regional audience across Illinois, Wisconsin, and Chicago with national and global reach.
Multi-Platform
Reached across YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and podcast platforms simultaneously.
What Partnership Looks Like

What You Get

Every partnership is different and built around what makes sense for your brand. Here's what CDF brings to the table across any arrangement.

01
Video Placement
Logo, verbal mention, or full title sponsorship on long-form studio sessions distributed across all four platforms.
02
Short-Form Content
Clips from sessions distributed as Reels, Shorts, and TikToks carrying your brand into the highest-discovery formats.
03
Podcast Integration
Jam w/ Jason interviews are pushed to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and all major streaming platforms. Your brand reaches listeners too.
04
Description Links
Your link and brand description in every video description across all platforms for the life of the content.
05
Social Tags
Tagged and collaboration-credited on all posts across YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok at publish.
06
Authentic Context
Your brand lives inside real content, not an ad slot. CDF's audience notices what's in the room because they care about what's in the room.
Why It Matters
100% Of Every Sponsorship
Goes Back Into The Platform.

When you partner with CDF, your dollars are not going to overhead or profit. Every sponsorship is reinvested directly into the platform: better production, more sessions, wider distribution, and more exposure for the independent artists who deserve it. Supporting CDF means supporting the artists on screen.
