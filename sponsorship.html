How It Works

Standard Terms

Payment
50% deposit due to begin production. Remaining 50% due upon delivery and confirmation of publish. CDF Music Group will send a formal invoice upon package selection.
Script Review
For any package including a spoken sponsor read, CDF Music Group provides script recommendations prior to filming. Your brand is welcome to review and approve all messaging before production begins.
Analytics
A full analytics report covering views, engagement, reach, and platform performance will be delivered at the conclusion of the campaign. Included with all packages at no additional cost.
Brand Assets
Please supply high-resolution logo files (PNG with transparent background), brand color codes, and any usage guidelines at least 7 business days prior to the scheduled recording date.
Content Window
All sponsored content remains active on all platforms for a minimum 30-day campaign window. Long-form YouTube content stays live indefinitely, continuing to deliver impressions beyond the campaign period.
Custom Packages
Looking for something different? Multi-month campaigns, remote session sponsorships, or custom integrations are all on the table. Reach out and we'll build something that fits.
