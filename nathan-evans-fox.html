About Nathan Evans Fox

Country on
His Own Terms

Nathan Evans Fox is an indie country singer-songwriter with a sharp pen and an authentic voice. His music sits at the intersection of honest storytelling and the kind of melody that sticks with you long after the song is over. He is the kind of writer who makes it sound easy, which is the hardest thing to do.

Fox came to CDF for the Jam w/ Jason interview and held his own in a long-form conversation about where his music comes from, what the indie country path looks like, and what drives him to keep creating outside the mainstream system. His answers were direct, his perspective earned, and his catalog backs up everything he says.

He is an artist building something real, one song at a time, and the CDF conversation gave listeners a genuine look at the person behind the music.
