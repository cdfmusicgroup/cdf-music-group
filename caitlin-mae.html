About Caitlin Mae

Nashville
Storyteller

Caitlin Mae is a Nashville-based, award-winning country singer-songwriter whose music has earned international acclaim, radio success, and a rapidly growing global fanbase. A graduate of Belmont University, she has taken her music from arena stages and major festivals to headline tours across the US, sharing the stage with country superstar Carrie Underwood.

Known for her honest storytelling and dynamic live performances, Caitlin Mae crafts songs that connect with listeners far beyond the stage. Her single "House Sitting" earned Single of the Year at the International Singer-Songwriter Association Awards, Best Modern Country Song at the World Songwriting Awards, and received Grammy consideration.

She has been featured on WSM Radio, the official home of the Grand Ole Opry, and continues to release new music while touring nationally and internationally. During her CDF session she performed her unreleased original "Flipside" live for one of the first times.
